KISAN ALERT _ VISHWAKARMA DEVS
STAGE SCRIPT — EXACTLY THREE SLIDES

Target pace: 115–120 words per minute with the marked pauses. Rehearse once with a timer; do not add an introduction.

SLIDE 1 — 0:00–0:55

Honourable Members, this challenge did not begin in a lab. Code for Communities connected six MPs and builders around four constituency-submitted problems. Kisan Alert came from Narasaraopet farmers.

The gap is national: India has 14.65 crore operational holdings; 86 percent are small or marginal. In the 2019 NSS survey, only 48.7 percent of agricultural households accessed technical advice—yet 89.5 percent of recipients adopted it. Average monthly income was ₹10,218, with ₹3,798 from crops. Pests, diseases and weeds cause about 30 percent average yield loss.

Farmers act on advice. The bottleneck is getting the right advice to the right plot in time.

SLIDE 2 — 0:55–2:05

KisanVaani closes that loop. A farmer can call, text, WhatsApp or share a crop photo in her own language. We combine crop and location with soil, Soil Health Card, sixteen-day weather, mandi prices and agronomy before AI answers.

A confidence and risk gate prevents bluffing: clear cases get one next action; high-risk or uncertain cases go to RSK, KVK or Kisan Call Centre experts.

Every interaction is structured. Five matching reports within a radius and seven days create a possible cluster. An officer verifies it before a targeted alert goes out.

This is deployed: twelve farmer-interface languages, fourteen crops and four live data rails.

We complement government. Kisan Sarathi already connects 2.95 crore farmers, 730-plus KVKs and 4,767 scientists. KisanVaani adds the configurable local loop: any-phone intake, structured triage and pattern detection.

SLIDE 3 — 2:05–3:00

Our ask is one block, one crop and one crop cycle in Narasaraopet—not a new scheme.

Your office unlocks the roster and consent, official IVR and SMS, a nodal officer, KVK or RSK experts and a district review slot. We bring Telugu onboarding, local configuration, safety rules, a weekly dashboard and an endline report.

The public scorecard measures reach, useful-advice time, expert resolution, cluster false positives, alerts acted on within forty-eight hours and cost per useful action. No invented yield claim.

If one cycle proves value, replicate across the district and seek integration with Kisan Sarathi, KCC and NPSS. If it does not, publish that too.

Nominate the block—and judge the evidence. Dhanyavaad.
